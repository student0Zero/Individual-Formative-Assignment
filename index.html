Why Community Matters

Community is the thread that brings people together. Community matters because it creates a sense of purpose, builds social connections, and offers a support network

Many different types of communities exist. Some groups have more of a structure, such as religious organizations, co-op organizations, parent-teacher associations, trade organizations, governments, and health care organizations. Others—like social networks, study groups, and groups of close friends—are more informal. Follow the link below to see a few types of community you can join.
